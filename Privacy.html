Privacy Policy for JSL FastLine

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

JSL FastLine (“the App”) is an offline-first social, communication, and utility platform designed to help users connect, share, and transact with or without internet connectivity. Protecting your privacy and safeguarding your data is a core priority. This Privacy Policy explains how JSL FastLine collects, stores, and uses information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

- Posts, messages, files, and marketplace listings created within the app. - Service requests and responses. We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Device location data for local discovery features

2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our platform, including:

  • App usage patterns and features accessed
  • Offline and online activity logs
  • Device information and connectivity status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our offline-first platform
  • Enable local discovery and peer-to-peer connections
  • Sync data when internet connectivity is available
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. When online connectivity is available, data may be synchronized with our secure servers. We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy or required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices
  • Export your data in a portable format
